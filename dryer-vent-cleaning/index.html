Why Dryer Vent Cleaning in Huntsville is a Safety Emergency

Lint is one of the most flammable materials in your home. Most homeowners don't realize the danger until it's too late.

3,000House fires per year from clogged dryer vents (U.S. Fire Administration)

The most dangerous part: Most lint accumulates in the middle and far sections of the dryer vent line — not in the trap you can reach. DIY cleaning misses most of it. Professional cleaning with a rotary brush reaches every inch.

Warning Signs

Signs Your Dryer Vent Needs Cleaning Now

If you notice any of these, call us today. A clogged dryer vent is a fire hazard that gets worse with every load.

  • Clothes take 2+ cycles to dry — most common sign of restricted airflow
  • Dryer feels very hot to the touch during operation
  • Burning smell when the dryer is running
  • Dryer shuts off mid-cycle due to overheating safety switch
  • Laundry room feels humid or hot while dryer is running
  • More than 12 months since last dryer vent cleaning
