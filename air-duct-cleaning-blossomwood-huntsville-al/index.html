Warning Signs

Signs Your Blossomwood Home Needs Air Duct Cleaning

If you are experiencing any of these in your Blossomwood home, it is time to schedule a professional cleaning.

Dust Around Vents
Visible dust or dark buildup around vent covers — a clear sign of heavy duct debris.
Allergy Symptoms Indoors
Sneezing, itchy eyes, or congestion that worsens at home — often caused by allergens in dirty ducts.
Musty or Stale Odors
Unpleasant smells when the AC or heat runs indicate mold or debris inside the ductwork.
High Energy Bills
Clogged ducts restrict airflow, forcing your HVAC to work harder and driving up monthly energy costs.
Never Been Cleaned
If you have owned your Blossomwood home 3+ years and never had duct cleaning, it is overdue.
Pets in the Home
Pet dander and hair accumulate rapidly in Alabama home ductwork — more frequent cleaning needed.
